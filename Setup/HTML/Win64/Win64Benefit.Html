How will 64-bit NT benefit your enterprise?
  1. 64 bits brings more productivity.

    When your applications run faster, more work gets done. Knowledge workers spend their time thinking and producing rather than waiting for the computer to finish its tasks.

  2. 64-bits can bring lower cost.

    Each server in your computing environment will support larger numbers of users and applications. Fewer servers will be needed to do the job. This translates directly into less complex and costly system and network management overhead — one of the highest costs in any computing system.

  3. 64-bits opens up new application opportunities.

    New applications will be designed without the barriers imposed by 32-bit systems today. Larger and faster Internet servers can be built. New graphics applications will make work easier and more enjoyable. Data intensive tasks that are impossible today can easily be done with the flexibility that 64-bit Windows NT will provide.
